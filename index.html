Fomoro

Train your deep learning models in the cloud.

Sign up for early access.

1. Push your model and dataset:

$ fomoro data push
$ fomoro model push

2. Train your model:

$ fomoro session start

3. Monitor progress:

$ fomoro session watch
Running [===-----------------] 14% (280/2000) ETA: 128.4s

4. Download checkpoints:

$ fomoro checkpoint pull

Read the docs as they develop, or contact us with any questions.
